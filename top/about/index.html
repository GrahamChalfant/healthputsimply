Health Put Simply

Teaching you to tell time, not build the watch

Hi, my name is Graham Chalfant, and this site is a way for me to share the things I have learned living with a therapist, nutritionist, doctor, and healthcare professional.

I grew up in Charlotte, NC, with a highly health-conscious family. As a child, I thought my parents were psychotic and despised their health choices. I still have trust issues with my mom after she tried to feed my zucchini brownies…

Although, over time, I have come to appreciate their efforts and adopted many of their health choices myself. I have also shaped my own health practices through different studies, podcasts, and books.

Becoming a healthier version of yourself both mentally and physically can be an elusive and daunting task. There is a flood of health information in the world, and much of it is not easy to understand.

I want to distil this information into digestible, bite-size pieces. Thereby helping you find what is essential and understanding it. I will share experiences my family, close friends, and I have had with various health practices. Additionally, I will summarize fascinating articles and podcasts which can help you live a healthier life.

This site is a way for me to share everything I have learned while trying to achieve my health goals.

Disclaimer: this website is intended for general information purposes only. This website does not offer medical advice, and claims of such are not being made. This website is a way to share past experiences and ideas produced by professionals and practitioners.
